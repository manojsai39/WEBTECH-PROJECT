Aha Adds Blockbusters To International Library

100% Telugu OTT entertainment app Aha has been making all the right noises recently. Along with acquiring hit content and releasing exclusive talk shows and movies, Aha has been focusing on increasing it’s base outside India as well. As an extension to this, Aha is adding many films exclusively for people outside India. Recent blockbuster of Power Star Pawan Kalyan will start streaming on Aha for people outside India from tomorrow. Other excellent titles like Sarileru Neekevvaru, Jaanu, V, Nishabdham and Falaknuma Das are also being added to the already existing library of 30 odd exclusive overseas streaming films. Team Aha seems to have struck the right formula with respect to positioning of it’s app.

#SSMB28: Mahesh Breaking His Rule!

Superstar Mahesh Babu, who usually shoots for one film at a once, has decided to break the rule for SSMB28. After Trivikram’s film, Mahesh will move onto SS Rajamouli’s magnum opus which is touted to be a Jungle-adventure film and has a fantasy layer to it.

Balakrishna’s Film Is Not A Multistarer: Anil Ravipudi

OTT Alert: Dhanush’s JT gets a release date

We are already aware that Dhanush’s Jagame Thanthiram opted for a direct OTT release instead of going for a theatrical run. Streaming giant Netflix has bought the digital rights of this movie. Now, the makers have officially announced the world premiere date of this movie. Jagame Thanthiram will have its world premiere on June 18 on Netflix. It will be available in both Tamil and Telugu languages. There is no update on whether it will be available in other languages too. Directed by Karthik Subbaraj, this gangster-comedy features Aishwarya Lekshmi as the female lead. Y Not Studios has bankrolled this project.

Terrific TRP Ratings For Uppena

Uppena was premiered on Star Maa a few days back. It got a tremendous response from the viewers and garnered a huge TRP rating of 18.5. The theatrical result and super hit musical album helped the movie to get this massive viewership. On the flip side, Vijay’s Master, which also had a world television premiere on Gemini TV, managed to get only 4.8 ratings. No Direct OTT Release For Narappa!

The Telugu remake of Asuran, Narappa, has been postponed. The makers made an official statement confirming the postponement of the film’s release which was initially scheduled for 14th May release. “In lieu of the pandemic, #Narappa will not be releasing on May 14th. A new theatrical date will be announced once we overcome this unprecedented crisis. Stay Safe,” tweeted Venkatesh Daggubati. It is learnt that the film’s producer Suresh Babu is keen on having a theatrical release for Narappa and he wants to release the film only in cinemas once the situation comes to normalcy. In the statement released to press it is said that the new release date of the film would be announced appropriately and the film would be released when the “time is right”. The film is directed by Srikanth Addala and it has Mani Sharma’s music.

Cinema Bandi Trailer: Everyone Is A Filmmaker At Heart

Telugu origin Bollywood directors duo Raj & DK are coming up with a straight Telugu film after a long time.
